PyPI · OCI · one binary

Your packages, served from next door.

velodex is a read-through cache and private registry in a single Rust binary. Point your package and container clients at it; the only change any of them needs is the URL. The first pull runs at upstream speed while the cache fills, every one after comes from your own disk.
